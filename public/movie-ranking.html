The Best Movies According to Sinead

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

Everything, Everywhere, All At Once

At first glance this is a film about a Chinese American woman who runs a laundromat with her husband and struggles to do her taxes and get along with her daughter.
It turns out to be fantastically weird and wonderful film about hope, connection, generational struggles within a family and the meaning of life as you choose it to be.
I was deeply inspired by this film and connected to the characters and the chaos.

Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

This film seems like it's just going to be another Spiderman movie and we've had our fair share of that so I wasn't expecting too much when I went to see it.
However, the creators of this Spiderman: Into the Spider-Verse pushed the boundaries of animation. It was so creative, fun, and artistic.
I was very inspired by the artistry of the film and moved by the characters and stories and how they weaved into one another.

The Greatest Showman

This film follows the story of people who are ostracized because they're considered "freaks" and P.T. Barnum who,
together with his family, creates the circus as a safe place for these people to be seen.
The whirlwind of the story and the beautiful original soundtrack and choreography are awe-inspiring.
